Online loan service

Business loans and personal loans of all types are available for qualified individuals and business owners. Our bank's loan application is easy, you can apply for online loan, and in many cases approval will be virtually instantaneous. We offer low interest rate loans for early customers!

Low Interest Rate Loans

Carnegie Financial Services bank works on the business motto of quick reliable online loan services. Our electronic banking's online loan service is offered as low interest rate loans for our first time customers. We cater to both personal loan and business loan seeking customers. To apply for a bank loan, fill out bank loan form and submit proof of collateral here.

Housing Loans

CFS bank loan service caters to housing loan seeking customers. Our housing loan service accepts a down payment of upto 20% on all types of housing loans. housing loans can be paid in installments of upto ten years for a fixed interest rate chosen at the time of applying for housing loans. Loan calculator will assist you with your housing loan decisions.

Business Loans

CFS bank loan service caters to business loan seeking enterprise customers as well. CFS bank's business loan service offers jumbo interest rates to for corporates applying for a business loan above 1 million USD. CFS business loans has a special category called Startup Business Loans to provide funding for early stage startups. loan calculator will assist you with your business loan strategies.

School Loans

CFS bank loan offers low interest rate loans for customers seeking loans for education purposes. In addition to the low interest rate loans for students, CFS bank loan also has a policy where in student will incurs only the principal amount for their debt until they graduate and will start incurring the low interest rate on their loans only after their graduation. Low interest rate Loan calculator will assist you in your student loan decision.

Auto Loans

CFS bank loan service caters auto loan and car loan seeking customers. CFS bank's car loan service accepts a down payment of upto 25% on all types of auto/car loans. Auto loans can be paid in installments of upto five years for a fixed interest rate chosen at the time of applying car loans. Loan calculator will assist you with your auto loan decisions.

Business loan and Personal loan refinancing and consolidation

CFS bank Loan online service offers its customers, the option for loan refinancing, thereby enabling paying off the debt from the first loan with another loan. The refinancing loan is offered at a lower interest rate and term. Large scale business loans are eligible for special corporate refinancing benefits too. To know more click Business Loan Benefits.
Our Bank loans can also be consolidated, where in the customers are allowed to combine several loans they have borrowed from us, into one bigger loan, eligible to be paid in in installments at a jumbo loan rate calculated using our Loan Calculator.
