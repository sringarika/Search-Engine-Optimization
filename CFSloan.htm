CFS Bank Online Loan - a low interest rate loan service

Business and personal loans of all types are available for qualified individuals and enterprises. Loan application is easy, you can apply online, and in many cases approval will be virtually instantaneous. We offer attractively low interest rate loans for early customers!

eLoan - An Online Personal Low Interest Rate Loan Service

Carnegie Financial Services offers quick reliable online loan services. Our electronic banking's online loan service is offered as low interest rate loans for our first time customers. We cater to both personal loan and business loan seeking customers. To apply for a loan fill out this form and submit proof of collateral here.

Housing Loans

CFS bank loan service caters to housing loan seeking customers. Our housing loan service accepts a down payment of upto 20% on all types of housing loans. housing loans can be paid in installments of upto ten years for a fixed interest rate chosen at the time of applying for housing loans. Here is a loan calculator that will assist you with your housing loan decisions.

Auto Loans and Car Loans

CFS bank loan service caters auto loan and car loan seeking customers. CFS bank's car loan service accepts a down payment of upto 25% on all types of auto/car loans. Auto loans can be paid in installments of upto five years for a fixed interest rate chosen at the time of applying car loans. Here is a loan calculator that will assist you with your auto loan decisions.

Business Loans and Corporate Finance

CFS bank loan service caters to business loan seeking enterprise customers as well. CFS bank's business loan service offers jumbo interest rates to for corporates applying for a business loan above 1 million USD. CFS business loans has a special category called Startup Business Loans to provide funding for early stage startups. Here is a loan calculator that will assist you with your business loan strategies.

School Loan and College Student Loan

CFS bank loan offers low interest rate loans for customers seeking loans for education purposes. eLoan service has special leeway for students, their student debt incurs only the principal amount until they graduate and will start incurring annual percentage rate of interest only after their graduation. Here is a loan calculator to assist you.

Loan refinancing and Consolidation loans for personal and business loans

CFS eLoan service offers its customers, the option for loan refinancing, thereby enabling paying off the debt from the first loan with another loan. The refinancing loan is offered at a lower interest rate and term. Large scale business loans are eligible for special corporate refinancing benefits too. To know more click Corporate Benefits.
Our eBank also offers the option of consolidation loans, where in the customers are allowed to combine several loans they have borrowed from us, into one bigger loan, eligible to be paid in in installments at a jumbo loan rate calculated using our Loan Calculator.
