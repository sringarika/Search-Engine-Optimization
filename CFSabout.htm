Our Motto : Your entire financial needs served online, anytime, anywhere, at the best possible rates. That’s Carnegie Financial Services!

Who We Are : Carnegie Financial Services (CFS) offers premier online banking services, known as ebanking. Our satisfied customers range from major corporations, such as Moffet Foods and Big Red Tractors, to wealthy individuals and to large numbers of professionals, educators, and students of all income ranges.

Our Founder : The late Dr. Harry Q. Bovik had the vision to make all banking transactions electronic way back in the 1980’s, before the web came into existence. His first business, Long Range Transactions, was acquired by Manufacturer’s Hanover in 1990, and he dedicated the proceeds to making CFS a reality. After many difficult years, Dr. Bovik made eBanking really work. Now CFS is proud to be the premier pure eBank!
